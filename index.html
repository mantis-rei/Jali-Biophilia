OUR STORY

Where Mt Jali Meets
Your Spirit

Nestled in the Gashyushya valley beneath the iconic Mt Jali (2,078m) — one of Kigali's most majestic peaks — Jali Biophilia is Rwanda's premier nature-integrated wellness destination. Our story begins with the ancient legacy of King Cyilima I Rungwe and the timeless bond between the Rwandan people and their thousand hills.

Here, biophilia — our innate human connection to nature — comes alive through guided forest walks, open-air wellness, farm-to-table nutrition, and AI-powered personal coaching. Every trail, every breath of eucalyptus-scented air, every hornbill call is an invitation to reconnect with what matters most.
