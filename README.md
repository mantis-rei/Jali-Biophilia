# Jali Biophilia — Where Nature Meets Your Potential

A premium digital gateway for nature-integrated wellness tourism beneath Mt Jali in Kigali, Rwanda.

## 🌿 Overview

**Jali Biophilia** is Rwanda's premier destination for nature-integrated wellness tourism, nestled in the Gashyushya valley beneath the iconic Mt Jali (2,078m). This immersive digital experience showcases the perfect fusion of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and modern wellness technology.

### Key Features

- **Hero Section**: Full-screen landscape imagery showcasing the breathtaking vistas of Mt Jali
- **Introduction**: Storytelling that connects Rwanda's royal heritage with modern biophilic wellness
- **Services Grid**: Four core offerings - Nature Watching, Family Park, Hiking Trails, and Tourism Camp
- **Interactive 3D Map**: Explore the terrain with draggable, zoomable 3D visualization
- **Trail Network**: Three difficulty levels from gentle valley loops to summit challenges
- **Community Voices**: Testimonials from local guides, conservationists, and visitors
- **Impact Dashboard**: Real-time metrics on environmental and community impact
- **Sport Mode**: AI-powered wellness coaching with personalized workout plans
- **Booking System**: Integrated reservation system for all experiences
- **Responsive Design**: Fully optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop devices

## 🏞️ Experiences Offered

### Nature Watching
Discover 120+ bird species, indigenous flora, and rare mammals through guided observation decks and expert-led forest walks.

### Family Park
Natural playgrounds, picnic areas, educational trails, and safe zones designed for families with children of all ages.

### Hiking
Three trail options from Valley Loop (2km) to Summit Challenge (6km) with guided tours and equipment rental.

### Tourism Camp
Camping plots, glamping tents, bonfire areas, and star-gazing experiences under the Rwandan night sky.

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

- **Frontend**: HTML5, TailwindCSS, JavaScript
- **3D Visualization**: Three.js (r128)
- **Animations**: GSAP (GreenSock Animation Platform)
- **Typography**: Space Grotesk, Inter, JetBrains Mono
- **Smooth Scrolling**: Lenis.js
- **Responsive**: Mobile-first design with breakpoints at 768px
